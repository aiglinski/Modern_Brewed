Brewed on Brady

Located in the heart of Historic Brady Street, the warm and homey "Brewed Cafe" invites you to relax with an excellent quality coffee (roasted by our good friends at Anodyng Coffee Roasting Company) and an array of delicious foods ranging from homemade bakery to our signature vegetarian chili, sanwiches and spreads.

We strive to offer a comfortable, friendly setting where all are welcome to meet and freely discuss the significant issues, great and small, of our daily lives in the time honored tradition of the Brady Street Area.

As always, we value your business and friendship, and will continue to make Brewed your favorite cafe!

Thanks from all of us at Brewed!
